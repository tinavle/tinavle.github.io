About this Project

What: This project explores how European religious missionaries shaped education in colonized communities in Africa and Asia, drawing from primary texts such as missionaries’ reports, students’ schoolwork, and interviews. While exploring this site, consider:

  • What are the purposes of education, and who decides?
  • What kinds of knowledge are valued or devalued, and by whom?
  • How does literacy empower or control?
  • How do colonial views of gender and morality impact education?
